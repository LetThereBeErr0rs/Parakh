Privacy & Security

How your enterprise data is handled.

  • Zero Tracking: No personal analyst metrics or IP trails are stored across verifications.
  • Ephemeral Processing: Incoming text, URLs, and Images are stored purely in-memory strictly for document chunking and vector embedding generation.
  • Local Fallbacks: Core indexing operates natively via offline FAISS databases (Liar Dataset vector mappings).
  • Encrypted Transport: When falling back to enterprise LLMs (Gemini Multi-Modal arrays), data is securely tunneled over TLS. No training caching is enabled on the downstream LLM endpoints.
